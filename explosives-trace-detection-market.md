<p><p>The Explosives Trace Detection market comprises two primary types: Body Detection Technology and Trace Detection Technology. Body Detection Technology involves screening individuals for explosive residues on their clothing or skin, often using advanced sensors and sampling methods. In contrast, Trace Detection Technology focuses on identifying minute traces of explosives in various environments, like luggage or cargo, utilizing techniques such as ion mobility spectrometry and swabbing methods. Both technologies aim to enhance security by ensuring quick and accurate detection of potential threats.</p></p>
