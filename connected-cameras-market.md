<p><ul><li>Wi-Fi</li><li>Near Field Communications</li><li>Bluetooth</li><li>Internet Protocol</li><li>A Combination of All Four</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Connected cameras are categorized based on their connectivity options. Wi-Fi allows for high-speed data transfer and remote access. Near Field Communications (NFC) enables quick pairing with smartphones for easy sharing. Bluetooth offers lower power consumption and direct, close-range connections. Internet Protocol (IP) cameras utilize internet connectivity for remote monitoring and control. A combination of all four types enhances functionality, allowing users to choose the most suitable connection method based on their needs, providing flexibility in usage and application.</p></p>
