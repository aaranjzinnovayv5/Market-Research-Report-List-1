<p><p>The methyl orthoformate market is primarily categorized into two methods of production: the Hydrocyanic Acid Method and the Sodium Metal Method. The Hydrocyanic Acid Method involves the reaction of methyl alcohol with hydrocyanic acid, resulting in high purity methyl orthoformate. Conversely, the Sodium Metal Method employs sodium metal to facilitate the synthesis, typically yielding a different purity level and cost structure. Both methods serve various industrial applications, impacting the overall market dynamics and pricing strategies.</p></p>
